Sizzling Samurai Sushi

Description

Sail into the flaming heart of a volcano with our Sizzling Samurai Sushi. This is sushi like you've never seen before, ablaze with the fires of Mount Fuji and bursting with the spirit of a samurai.

Ingredients

  • Sushi-grade rice, harvested by moonlight
  • Nori sheets, whispered into existence by sea sprites
  • Fresh seafood, brave and bold, ready to face the inferno
  • Crisp veggies, fresh from the imperial gardens

Steps

  1. Summon sushi-grade rice onto a nori sheet with samurai precision.
  2. Arrange seafood and veggies like warriors ready for battle.
  3. Roll the sushi with the might of a sumo wrestler.
  4. Set it ablaze with the fires of Mount Fuji, then extinguish with a swift samurai slash.
